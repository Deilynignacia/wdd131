A Christmas Carol: The Musical

Site Planning

SITE NAME

Site Name: A Christmas Carol: The Musical

Description:The proposed website will serve to inform, promote, and provide updates on all matters related to the Christmas musical. The performances are scheduled for this December at various Church's facilities across the Valparaíso region, Chile.

Optional domain availability:achristmascarolmusical.org

SITE PURPOSE

This musical represents a powerful opportunity for people from all ages to come together and remember the true meaning of the most joyous season of the year, and creating an accessible and user-friendly website, would allow for the event to be properly promoted, providing it with a larger audience. By applying responsive web design practices and the JavaScript functionalities learned through this course, the website promises to be an engaging hub of information of everything related to the musical.

SCENARIOS

Questions that site visitors might ask:

  • Where and when can I see the performances of the musical "A Christmas Carol"?
  • How can I audition to be part of the cast of the musical?
  • What is the story behind "A Christmas Carol"?
  • Are there photos or videos of the "behind the scenes" of the rehearsals and preparation for the musical?

COLOR SCHEMA

A color that evokes a festive and traditional Christmas atmosphere.

  • Primary Color (Christmas Red): #821616 - Will be used for key section backgrounds, important accents, and decorative borders.
  • Secondary Color (Dark Green): #2e422c - Will be used for content section backgrounds, headings, and some elements.
  • Accent Color (Gold/Ivory): #A79573 - Will be used for borders, decorative lines, links, and text accents.
  • Background Color (Cream/Light): #F8F4ED - Will be ued for the general site background.
  • Text Color (Dark): #1B1B1B - Will be used for the main text in paragraphs and subheadings (Almost black).

TYPOGRAPHY

The font selection aims to balance readability with a Christmas aesthetic.

  • Headings (H1, H2, H3): "Playfair Display", serif. Chosen for its elegant and classic style that aligns with the Victorian aestethic of the musical. It will be used for the main headings and titles.
  • Body Text (Paragraphs, Lists): "Open Sans", sans-serif. Selected for its readability. It will be use for long blocks of text.
