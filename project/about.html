Lighting the World Through Stories

Our goal is to share the timeless message of hope and redemption, and the joy of Christmas. A Christmas Carol is more than just a theatrical production; it is an opportunity to bring the community together, both members and friends of the Church, to celebrate and rejoice.

One More Time!

For the second year in a row, our Directors, Deilyn and Meilhyn Zamudio bring a cherished Christmas story to life. After last year's succesful production of "Journey to Betlehem: The Musical", that was performed at El Belloto Stake. This year, being the first ever collaboration with Institute. We are thrilled to shared this classic with our audience, and continue this tradition for many Christmases to come.

About the Cast

We are looking for people who want to have an unforgettable experience and shine on stage to bring to life this joyfull story! It doesn't matter if you have experience or not, there is a place for you!

We invite you to audition for some of the following parts:

  • Leading Roles: Iconic characters such as the miser Ebenezer Scrooge, his kind clerk Bob Cratchit, or the mysterious Ghosts of Christmas.
  • Supporting Roles: Secondary characters who complement the story and bring it to life, such as the Cratchit family, the Fezziwig family, or Scrooge's nephew, Fred.
  • Chorus and Dance Ensemble: Singers and dancers who fill the stage with the energy of London's streets, giving voice and movement to the community.
